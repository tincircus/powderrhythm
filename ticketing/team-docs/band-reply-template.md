# Band Inquiry Reply — Template

For bands emailing wanting to play. We're not booking the coming year yet, but we want every reply to feel warm and keep the door open. Copy, fill the brackets, send. Keep it human. Tweak per band.

_(House style: no em-dashes, go easy on exclamation points, no marketing-speak.)_

---

## Standard reply

**Subject:** Thanks for reaching out to Powder Rhythm

Hi [Band / Artist name],

Thanks so much for thinking of Powder Rhythm, and for wanting to play our room. It really means a lot that you reached out.

Here's the honest situation. We're a small record shop and venue in Baker City that's just getting the live side going, and we're not booking shows for the coming year yet. We didn't want to leave you on read or give you a vague "maybe," so we'd rather be straight with you.

What we'd love to do is keep you on our radar for when the calendar opens up. If you send a couple links (music, socials, anything that gives us a feel for your live set), we'll add you to the list and reach back out when we start booking.

Thanks again for the note, and for making the kind of music that gets people in a room together. We hope to share a night with you before too long.

Warmly,
[Your name]
Powder Rhythm, Baker City OR
